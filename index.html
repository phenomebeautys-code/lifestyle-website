Guides

Know before you go

Quick tips to get the most out of your appointment.

Waxing prep

Hair should be at least 5mm long (about 2–3 weeks of growth). Avoid sun exposure and tanning 24 hours before. No retinoids or exfoliants 48 hours prior.

After your wax

Avoid tight clothing, hot showers, swimming, and sunbeds for 24 hours after your wax. Use a gentle, fragrance-free moisturiser to keep skin calm and hydrated.

Gel nail care

Avoid soaking hands for the first 24 hours after application. Wear gloves for cleaning and use cuticle oil daily to keep the skin and nails healthy and extend the life of your gels.

Tint aftercare

Keep lashes and brows dry for 24 hours after tinting. Avoid oil-based makeup removers around the eye area, as these can fade the tint faster. Results typically last 4–6 weeks.
